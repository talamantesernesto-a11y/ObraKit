# Florida SB 658 Update (CRITICAL)
## Effective July 1, 2025

---

## What Changed

Florida Senate Bill 658 made the following changes to Fla. Stat. S 713.20:

### Before SB 658 (prior to July 2025)
- Florida lien waiver forms needed to be "substantially similar" to statutory templates
- ObraKit used a generic template for FL (same as TX, NY)

### After SB 658 (July 1, 2025 - NOW IN EFFECT)
- All lien waiver forms must be **IDENTICAL** to the forms prescribed in Fla. Stat. S 713.20(4) and (5)
- No party may require a lienor to use a form different from the statutory ones (S 713.20(6))
- Payment methods expanded beyond checks (ACH, wire, digital payments now recognized)

## Impact on ObraKit

**Our current Florida template is NON-COMPLIANT.** We are using a generic template that does not match the statutory forms.

We need to:
1. Create a dedicated Florida template (like we did for CA and GA)
2. Use the exact statutory language from Fla. Stat. S 713.20(4) (progress payment) and S 713.20(5) (final payment)
3. Support the expanded payment method language
