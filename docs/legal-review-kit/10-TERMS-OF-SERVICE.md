The documents generated by ObraKit are based on our understanding of applicable state laws and industry standards as of the date of generation. Laws change frequently, and ObraKit cannot guarantee that any document is fully compliant with the laws of your state at the time of use.

**YOU ARE SOLELY RESPONSIBLE** for reviewing all documents generated by ObraKit before signing, sending, or relying on them. We strongly recommend that you consult with a licensed attorney in your state to review any lien waiver before execution, particularly for:
- Unconditional waivers (which permanently waive your lien rights)
- Final payment waivers (which waive all remaining rights on a project)
- Waivers involving amounts greater than $50,000
- Any waiver you do not fully understand

## AI VALIDATION DISCLAIMER

ObraKit uses artificial intelligence to review waiver details and flag potential issues (such as amounts exceeding contract values or mismatched waiver types). This AI review is provided as a convenience only and does NOT constitute legal advice. The AI may miss issues, produce incorrect warnings, or fail to flag problems that a licensed attorney would catch.

You should NOT rely on ObraKit's AI validation as a substitute for professional legal review.

## STATE-SPECIFIC NOTICES

ObraKit generates lien waivers using templates designed to comply with applicable state statutes. However:

- In states that require specific statutory forms (Arizona, California, Florida, Georgia, Massachusetts, Michigan, Mississippi, Missouri, Nevada, Texas, Utah, and Wyoming), the enforceability of a lien waiver depends on strict compliance with statutory requirements. ObraKit makes reasonable efforts to match statutory forms but cannot guarantee exact compliance.

- In states that require notarization (Mississippi and Wyoming), documents generated by ObraKit are NOT valid until notarized by a licensed notary public.

- In states where electronic signatures may not be accepted for lien waivers, you may need to print and sign the document by hand.
