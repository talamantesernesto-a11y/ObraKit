# ObraKit Legal Review Kit

## What is ObraKit?

ObraKit is a SaaS platform that helps construction subcontractors generate, sign, and send lien waiver documents. The platform currently serves 5 states and is expanding to cover all 50 states.

## What We Need From You

We need you to review the waiver templates ObraKit generates to confirm they:

1. **Comply with statutory requirements** in the 12 states that mandate specific lien waiver forms
2. **Use correct legal language** in states without mandatory forms
3. **Properly classify waiver types** (conditional vs. unconditional, progress vs. final)
4. **Include all required fields and notices** per state statute
5. **Identify any liability risks** for ObraKit as the document generator
